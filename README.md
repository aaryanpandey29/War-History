# War-History

This is a battle based website where in due time, different battles shall be described along with tactics and outcomes.

Be free to contribute via the issues tab and raising pull requests for that issue.

#⚔️ Battle Archive

A military history website that breaks down battles into Before → During → After phases and allows readers to understand why a battle was won or lost in under 60 seconds.

#📖 Overview

Battle Archive analyzes battles through:

Force composition
Logistics
Terrain
Command decisions
Unit quality
Battle actions
Casualties
Strategic consequences

The goal is not to tell the story of a battle.

The goal is to explain why the outcome happened.

#🎯 Core Philosophy

Every battle is analyzed through three questions:

Before Battle
Who had the advantage?
Which army was stronger?
Which commander was better prepared?
During Battle
What happened?
What was the turning point?
Which decisions mattered?
After Battle
What were the losses?
What changed militarily?
What were the long-term consequences?
